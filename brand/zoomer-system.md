# Zoomer System

The Zoomer is Vibrant's proprietary testing methodology. Ten diagnostic categories. All major body systems. One integrated lab.

---

## Zoomer Naming Convention

Each Zoomer name is formatted as a compound word:

- **[System]** in heavier weight + **Zoomer** in regular weight
- Combined as one word (no space)
- "Zoomer" always capitalized

## Zoomer Architecture (Four Tiers)

Each tier has a parenthetical identity that makes it memorable for practitioners.

### CRITICAL - "The Drivers"
Highest-stakes risks that determine longevity and urgency of care.
- **CardioZoomer** - Heart
- **NeuralZoomer** - Neural/Brain
- Monitoring: Dynamic (ongoing)

### FUNCTIONAL - "The Levers"
Modifiable physiologic systems driving symptoms and long-term outcomes.
- **GutZoomer**
- **HormoneZoomer**
- **CellularZoomer**
- **ImmuneZoomer**
- Monitoring: Dynamic (ongoing)

### ENVIRONMENTAL - "The Controllables"
Internal and external exposures that provoke immune response.
- **ToxinZoomer**
- **FoodZoomer**
- **NutrientZoomer**
- Monitoring: Stable (as needed during treatment)

### FOUNDATIONAL - "The Guide"
Establishes a baseline health map to guide prevention and next steps.
- **FoundationZoomer**
- Monitoring: Dynamic (repeat annually)

- Each Zoomer has a unique circular icon

- Icon sits within a colored circle matching the Zoomer's assigned color
- Icon interior designs are unique per Zoomer

## Zoomer Card System

### White Cards (Standard)
- White rounded-rectangle cards in a grid layout
- Card structure (top to bottom):
  1. Zoomer Name (bold category + "Zoomer" in lighter weight, stacked)
  2. White space
  3. Zoomer icon (bottom-left area)
  4. Descriptive text

### Full-Flood Color Cards (Navigation)
- Used "when necessary" for easy navigation between tests
- Bottom section flooded with Zoomer's signature color
- Icon shown in monochrome/knockout on color field
- Upper portion remains white with Zoomer name

### Grid Layout
- Row 1: Nutrient, Gut, Cardio, Cellular
- Row 2: Hormone, Immune, Neural, Foundational
- Row 3: Toxin, Food
